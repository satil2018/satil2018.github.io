About Us

We care about our clients.

SATIL KNITWEAR (PVT.) LTD. is a BSCI, & SEDEX certified 100% export oriented knit garments factory established in the year of 2006. The company has knitting section, garments section with cutting, sewing & finishing and a printing factory. We take pride in our dedication to achieve the best possible results for our respective clients.
